Current State of Knowledge

Most people have misconceptions about trading in the stock or futures market:

  • It's just like gambling
  • It's a "get-rich-quick" scheme
  • Just "buy low, sell high"
  • Anyone can do it with minimal effort

These oversimplified views lead many traders to lose their capital. The reality is far more complex and requires specific skills and mindsets to navigate successfully.

Perception

  • Easy money
  • Exciting and thrilling
  • Quick profits

Reality

  • Complex strategies
  • Emotional discipline
  • Risk management
